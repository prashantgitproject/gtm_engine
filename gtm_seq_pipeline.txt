# GTM / Top of Funnel — Sequential Pipeline
**Client:** Wardell · AT Enterprises
**Version:** v0.2 · Prashant handoff · Apr 2026
**Playbook:** ADP / Paychex Displacement

---

## Overview

A five-stage sequential pipeline that takes a sales playbook and CRM as inputs, defines ICP and signal rules, enriches matched accounts via Bitscale and Clay, and produces hyperpersonalized multi-channel outreach sequences routed through BCP (brand/voice layer).

---
